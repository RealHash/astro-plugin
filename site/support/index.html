What to include in your message

For bug reports

Include the Google Docs™ context, what you clicked, the symbol involved, and any visible error message or screenshot.

For access issues

Include the Google account type, whether the add-on was installed manually or through a test deployment, and what step failed.

For policy questions

Reference the relevant page or requirement and include the URL you are reviewing so the response can be precise.

For commercial questions

At present there is no public billing flow. If you need clarification on planned pricing or rollout status, mention that explicitly.
